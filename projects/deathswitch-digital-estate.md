# DeathSwitch — Digital Estate Platform

**Date:** 2025-05-29
**Status:** 💡 Idea
**Tags:** #saas #fintech #crypto #estate-planning #death-tech

## Concept

Everyone dies. Nobody plans for their digital life. DeathSwitch auto-delivers messages, assets, and credentials to heirs after verified inactivity.

## The Problem

- Crypto wallets with $100B+ lost forever
- Passwords, subscriptions, cloud storage, domains — all abandoned
- Current solutions are terrible — greenfield opportunity

## Features

- Inactivity detection → trigger delivery chain
- Encrypted vault for credentials, wallets, messages
- Multi-factor death verification (trusted contacts, legal docs)
- Auto-deliver to designated heirs
- Subscription management — cancel/pass on services

## Revenue Model

- $9.99/mo × 100M+ people who think about this
- Premium tiers for crypto-heavy users, estate lawyers
- B2B: white-label for financial advisors, law firms

## Moat

- Trust & encryption (hard to switch once you commit)
- Legal partnerships
- Network effects (heirs become users)
