# ComplianceAI — Auto-Updating Regulatory Engine

**Date:** 2025-05-29
**Status:** 💡 Idea
**Tags:** #saas #compliance #ai #regulatory #b2b

## Concept

Regulations change weekly across 190 countries. Small businesses get fined. ComplianceAI monitors every regulatory body worldwide and auto-generates compliance checklists per industry.

## The Problem

- Regulations change constantly, businesses can't keep up
- Small businesses can't afford compliance teams
- Fines are severe — ignorance isn't a defense
- $50B+ compliance market, nobody owns SMB

## Features

- AI monitors regulatory bodies worldwide (EPA, FDA, GDPR, etc.)
- Auto-generates compliance checklists per industry + location
- Slack/Teams alerts: "New EPA rule affects your business"
- Deadline tracking and action items
- Document generation (policies, notices, reports)

## Revenue Model

- $199-999/mo per business
- Industry-specific tiers (healthcare, fintech, food service)
- Enterprise: custom regulatory coverage
