# AgentOS — Billing & Monitoring for AI Agents

**Date:** 2025-05-29
**Status:** 💡 Idea ⭐ (top pick)
**Tags:** #saas #ai-agents #monitoring #billing #devtools

## Concept

"Datadog + Stripe for autonomous agents." AI agents are exploding. Nobody tracks what they cost, what they break, or how to control them. AgentOS does.

## The Problem

- AI agent costs are invisible and unpredictable
- No kill switches when agents go rogue
- No compliance logging for autonomous actions
- Every company deploying agents needs this yesterday

## Features

- Per-agent billing tracking (tokens, API calls, compute)
- Real-time cost dashboards per agent/team/org
- Kill switches — stop agents that exceed budgets or act erratically
- Compliance logging — full audit trail of agent actions
- Budget alerts and spending caps
- Agent performance metrics (success rate, latency, cost per task)

## Revenue Model

- $49-499/mo per org
- Usage-based pricing on top (per-agent, per-action)
- Enterprise tier for large deployments

## Why This Wins

- Agent wave is 2024-2026's gold rush — sell shovels
- Every company using agents feels this pain now
- Low competition — market is too new
- Natural upsell from monitoring → billing → governance
