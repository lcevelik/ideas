# CloneOS — Your AI Digital Twin

**Date:** 2025-05-29
**Status:** 💡 Idea
**Tags:** #saas #ai #digital-twin #productivity #agents

## Concept

Train an AI on your emails, voice, writing style, and decision patterns. It handles routine emails, meetings, and customer support as you. Not a chatbot — a persistent agent that gets smarter over time.

## The Problem

- Everyone wants more time
- Routine communications eat 60%+ of work hours
- Current AI assistants are generic, not personalized

## Features

- Train on your writing style, email history, voice samples
- Handle routine emails with your tone and decision patterns
- Join meetings, take notes, follow up as you
- Customer support that sounds like you
- Approval workflows — flag anything uncertain
- Gets smarter over time with feedback

## Revenue Model

- $29/mo individuals
- $299/mo professionals (lawyers, executives, sales)
- Enterprise: team clones for departments

## Open Questions

- Privacy — how to train without exposing sensitive data?
- Liability — who's responsible when the clone makes a mistake?
- Voice cloning — ethical concerns, consent
- How to handle the "uncanny valley" of almost-right responses?
- Data ownership — who owns the trained model?
