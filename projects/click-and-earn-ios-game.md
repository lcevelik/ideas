# Click & Earn — iOS Game

**Date:** 2025-05-29
**Status:** 💡 Idea
**Tags:** #ios #game #mobile #monetization #clicker

## Concept

An iOS clicker/idle game where players earn real money (or redeemable currency) after accumulating massive amounts of clicks. The hook: every click counts toward a real payout.

## The Hook

- Standard clicker game loop (satisfying, addictive)
- But the twist — after X million clicks, you actually get paid
- Creates a "is this real?" viral loop
- Players grind because there's a tangible reward at the end

## Monetization Options

- **Ads** — rewarded video for bonus clicks, interstitials between sessions
- **In-app purchases** — click multipliers, auto-clickers, boosters
- **Sponsorships** — brands sponsor milestone rewards
- **Affiliate** — players unlock deals/offers at certain thresholds
- **Revenue share** — ad revenue funds the payouts (sustainable loop)

## Key Mechanics

- Click = +1 counter (with satisfying haptics/sounds)
- Milestones unlock multipliers, auto-clickers, prestige resets
- Real payout threshold is intentionally very high (millions of clicks)
- Social features — leaderboards, share your click count
- Daily bonuses, streaks, FOMO mechanics

## Why It Could Work

- Clicker games are proven (Cookie Clicker, AdVenture Capitalist)
- "Get paid to play" is a powerful hook for casual gamers
- Viral potential — "I actually got paid from this game"
- Ad revenue can fund payouts profitably if threshold is high enough
- Low dev cost — simple mechanics, mostly UI + backend

## Risks

- Apple App Store policies on real-money payouts
- Payment processing fees eat into small payouts
- Regulatory — gambling/lottery classification?
- Users quitting before payout threshold (engagement problem)
- Sustainability — can ad revenue actually cover payouts?

## Open Questions

- Real cash vs gift cards vs in-app currency that converts?
- Payout threshold — what's the sweet spot? (1M clicks? 10M?)
- Apple's stance on apps that pay real money
- Legal review needed for gambling/lottery laws by country
- Backend — track clicks server-side to prevent cheating
- Viral mechanic — referral bonuses for inviting friends?
