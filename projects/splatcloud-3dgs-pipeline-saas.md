# SplatCloud — 3DGS Pipeline-as-a-Service

**Date:** 2025-05-29
**Status:** 💡 Idea
**Tags:** #3dgs #saas #virtual-production #business #pipeline

## The Opportunity

The $1M+ opportunity: everyone's building splat viewers (SuperSplat, Polycam, Luma). Nobody's building the **production pipeline** — the boring infrastructure that studios actually need.

## What Already Exists (from current work)

- COLMAP → Brush/gsplat training automation
- Batch processing, quality presets, gallery
- Camera tracking, PLY export, viewer
- Image sorting algorithm
- Gallery upload pipeline (planned)

## What's Missing in the Market

1. **Enterprise 3DGS asset management** — versioning, team collaboration, approval workflows for VP studios
2. **Automated quality control** — PSNR/SSIM/LPIPS benchmarks per splat, auto-reject bad scans
3. **Unreal/Unity integration** — push splats directly into game engines (nobody does this well)
4. **Multi-splat scene composition** — combine 10-50 splats into one virtual set (VP studios need this)

## Business Model

| Tier | Price | Target |
|------|-------|--------|
| SaaS | $99-499/mo per seat | Individual artists, small studios |
| Per-splat | $5-25 per scan | Volume processing, freelancers |
| Enterprise | $5K-50K/yr | Sony, Epic, Netflix-tier studios |

## Why This Wins

- Already built ~80% of the tech
- 20 years of VP relationships = instant distribution
- Studios trust someone who speaks their language
- Sony DMPC lab credibility
